About Laura

I’m a designer and undergraduate philosophy student; with a background in architecture and art theory. I have a skillset that allows to me work on conceptual development with a focus on branding and visual design.

I’m interested in time tracking, planning and tools for thought; in the synthetic properties of diagrams applied to interfaces; and the collage like nature of kitsch.
In the long-term, I want to fully integrate my academic work with my design work.

I love learning and I optimize for being an active learner: creating projects to integrate knowledge. I’m also building small products as side projects, in order to teach myself product design.

Currently I’m working freelance creating coherent visuals from brand identity to interfaces. I enjoy working on a small team and I'm keen to jump into uncharted waters and learn something new.
I am available for remote opportunities and collaborations.
