About Laura

I believe that technology and design plays an important role in creating possibilities for our world.

I'm commited to design and create for a diverse, plural and expansive future. Usually working with digital tools, making decisions informed by art, philosophy and theory.

I love learning and I optimize for being an active learner, creating projects and solving problems in a practical way.

Currently, I do freelance visual design. Before that, I went to architecture school. In the near future, I will be shifting into product design. My work is multidisciplinary at its core and is inspired by diagrams, kitsch and geometry.

Outside of work, I'm an undergraduate philosophy student, doing research on Deleuze. I’m a huge fan of black coffee. I do roller skating, I’m usually drawing, making crafts or hiking. I love planners and tools for time management.

What is this site about

This site went through many iterations before coming into its actual form.

Took me a while to understand that I wanted to avoid following common patterns and guidelines from the 874 million articles about portfolios on the internet.

What I'm saying here is "this is what I do, what I enjoy and how I think".

This is a digital space I created for myself. A place for intentional experimentation. What I call a "proto-folio".

It also a place to share my work, my thinking and my design process. Yes, a portfolio.

I am available for remote freelance design opportunities and open for collaborations.
