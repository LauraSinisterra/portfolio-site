Dating app

Product design

This is an experimental page. A way to show what I'm currently working on and my process in personal projects. I want this to be a dynamic portfolio site.
At the moment, my main personal project is a dating app that connects people based on their interests.
Updated august 2020

I want to combine the best aspects of existing dating services but with another focus: connections based on interests and emotional wellbeing.

Users will be able to discover and connect with compatible people, based on the music they like, their taste in movies, their hobbies or what was the last book they read.

They will be able to start a conversation based on something they already have in common and share content on their profiles.

My personal goal is to deliver a functional prototype and create a complete visual design.

Research
I started by having casual conversations about dating and taking notes on them. This was an entertaining way to test my assumptions, to learn new things and understand what people think about dating apps in a less structured way.

I organized my notes and highlighted with color codes, making it easier to scan for information and focusing on what was important for my research. My main questions were:

What is surprising or unexpected information about dating apps?
What are the most important aspects of connecting online?

Survey
With this survey, I highlighted the important information and compared to what I learned from my casual research.

After seeing the responses, I realized some of the questions were biased. It wasn’t possible to change them at this point, so I decided to advance on the project and make a new survey later on.
Using what I learned, I'm sure I'm going to be able to make better questions and understand my goals better.

What’s next?
I decided to use a different approach to personas, instead of multiple ones, I’m focusing on single people with hobbies and defined interests. Without genders or demographics in mind.

I’m currently working on three user flows: onboarding, messaging and a general flow.

I’ll start making lo-fi wireframes and sketching basic functionalities and features.
