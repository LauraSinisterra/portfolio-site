What is this site about

This site went through many iterations before coming into its actual form. It was a site in potency for a long time.

The ideas were there, I knew them, I also knew something was off. Took me a while to understand that I was doing things for what I thought others were expecting from me. Repeating patterns and following guidelines from the 874 million articles about portfolios on the internet.

I'm aware that this is a site for others. But what I'm saying here is not "look at this thing I made for you" what I'm saying is "this is what I do, what I enjoy and how I think".

The plurality of my skills and interests move me in a different direction. I'm not saying that is a good direction, I avoid the false dilemma of good vs. bad. I'm still finding what my own direction is.

So this is a digital space I created for myself. A place for exploration, for finding out what is my design practice about. What I call a proto-folio.

It also a place to show my work, my thinking and my design process. Yes, a portfolio.

At the end, its main purpose is to find out how can I make that useful for you: reader, potential collaborator, co-creator, employer, co-worker, friend.
