Process overview

I worked with a team with an industrial design and a civil engineer.

My main work and principal contribution was the layout and the design of the space configurations.

The materiality of the project was based mostly on the local materials: brick and wood, traditional in the region.The goal was not to stand out but blend in, and focus on the interior space dynamics.

Because this is a house for an older couple, the accessibility aspect was very important, not for the current moment of construction but for the future.

The durability aspect of designing architecture demands thinking in the future aspects and in our future self. How are we going to navigate the space when our bodies change?

My approach to answer this question is to minimize the amount of stairs and prioritize ramps as the main form of vertical circulation inside the house.
